Grilled Cheese

Description ::

This recipe is for classic grilled cheese! Nothing is quite as comforting as the melty-cheesey goodness of grilled cheese! Consider serving alongside a bowl of warm tomato soup for an epic pairing!

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices Bread (classic white works best)
  • 1 Slice American Cheese
  • 2 Slices Cheddar Cheese
  • Room Temperature or Spreadable Butter

Steps

  1. Butter both sides of your slices of bread.
  2. Place one piece of bread in a skillet over medium-low heat.
  3. Layer a piece of cheddar, followed by a piece of American, topped with a piece of cheddar cheese over the bread in the pan.
  4. Place second piece of buttered bread top. Flip sandwich over once bottom has become golden brown.
  5. Cover sandwich with a lid to help the cheese melt while the second side cooks.
  6. Once second side has become golden, remove from heat and serve!
