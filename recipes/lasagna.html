Lasagna

Description ::

This recipe is for classic Italian lasagna! Nothing is quite as comforting as the melty-cheesey goodness of fresh-baked lasagna! (Although I think it tastes even better the next day!)

Ingredients

  • 1lb Packaged Lasagna Noodles
  • 1lb Uncooked Ground Beef
  • 1lb Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 1 Jar Pasta Sauce
  • 1 8oz Container of Ricotta Cheese
  • 1/2lb Shredded Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Brown ground beef and sausage in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
  3. Once meat has browned, stir in pasta sauce and simmer for 20 minutes over low heat.
  4. In a baking dish, layer a small layer of sauce on the bottom. Add a few dollops of the ricotta cheese before sprinkling mozzerella cheese and placing a layer of cooked noodles on top.
  5. Repeat the layering process until all noodles and sauce has been used.
  6. Bake at 400 degrees F until golden brown and cheese is bubbling. Let sit for 10 minutes to rest before serving.
